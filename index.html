About Me

Danielle Cavallo is a multi-talented individual. Danielle’s driven to educate and guide her clients to make sound financial decisions when faced with retirement planning, wealth accumulation and lifetime income strategies. She takes pride in being a good listener and gains keen insight into the needs of individuals and businesses alike while building long lasting relationships based on trust and integrity. Danielle graduated from Caldwell University, New Jersey in 2015 with a Bachelor of Science degree in Marketing. Always enterprising, during her college years she maintained an active campus and community presence, volunteering for various philanthropic activities. Danielle also maintained outstanding academic achievement and was inducted into the Kappa Gamma Pi National Honors Society. In her junior year she co-founded a private computer service company where she oversaw marketing, advertising and client acquisition while also assisting in product development. Danielle comes from a family of financial planners and engineers and grew up surrounded by creativity, critical thinking, effective communication, and a collaborative spirit. As she continued her education, Danielle’s passion to help people fueled her interest in financial services and she obtained her producer license. She began her professional career in the industry with New York Life where she honed her skills in leadership and relationship building while gaining industry knowledge and experience. Danielle’s strong sense of community has kept her actively involved as a Team Volunteer of the American Cancer Society, participating in the Relay for Life for over 21 years. She leads an active lifestyle and enjoys playing basketball, softball and golf. She also practices Muay Thai and has a side passion for quantum physics. Danielle resides in Nashville, TN.
